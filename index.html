School Library

The St. Xavier's school has a innovative library.

The Libarary has a lot of books includinng comics ans syllabus books for every class. Other than these, this library also has an innovative design and we are allowed to borrow books for two weeks and its the choice of children that they want to borrow one book or two. If the book borrowed will be lost then you wil have to pay the amount and afterwards if you finnd the book then it will be yours. If the books will not be returnrd on time then the student will be charged 10 rs. per day. Rest rules are mentioned in the diary.

In the front, chairs are for audience to sit down and watch the stage performance. The special item in the morning prayer includes an act or a play or it can be a song or science experiment, that is als performed on this stage only.
